Choose your accommodation

We know everyone has different preferences when it comes to accommodation. That's why we provide three types of accommodation. When you book your course, let us know which accommodation type you prefer and we'll send you a list of options, all pre-checked for availability at the time of your course.

Home stay, full board

Enjoy the warm hospitality of a South African family when you choose home stay. You'll have the opportunity to speak English every day with your host family which will help you speed up your learning. With full board, all your meals are provided.

Home stay, half board

This is our most popular choice as you will still enjoy the benefits of staying with a host family, however, only breakfast will be provided. Many students prefer to take care of their own meals throughout the day.

Self-catering, non-shared

Some students may prefer to stay in their own apartment. We have many options for this type of accommodation and, depending on your preferences, you could rent a small studio apartment, or a larger cottage or house.
