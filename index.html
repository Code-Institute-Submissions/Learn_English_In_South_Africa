Why study in South Africa?

World-class courses

All the courses you will find here are accredited by the Language FE Board and classes are small to ensure individual attention. When you book with us, we will guide you through the process of choosing the best course to suit your needs.

The experience

Enjoy everything South Africa has to offer including our world-famous safaris, stunning beaches and areas of natural beauty. Enjoy our wine farms, take a tour through our cities or give back through our volunteering opportunities.

Accelerate your learning

One of the best ways to accelerate your learning is to speak English every day and as often as possible. Living in South Africa while you learn, you'll have plenty of opportunities to practise speaking English in your daily activies and with your host family.

Get started in 3 easy steps

We help you find the best course for you, book your accommodation and choose your experiences in 3 easy steps.

Step 1
Find your course

First, find the course that best suits your needs. When you book, we will hold your place while you finalise the next two steps.

Step 2
Choose your accommodation

Next, browse our accommodation options and choose the type you prefer. We'll send you a list of accommodation pre-checked for availability at the time of your chosen course.

Step 3
Choose your experiences

Now you get to start planning all the activities you want to do while in South Africa. Let us know which types you are interested in and we'll send you a menu of activities to pick from.
